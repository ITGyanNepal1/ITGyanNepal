About IT Gyan Nepal

IT Gyan Nepal, founded in 2082 B.S., is a pioneering educational institution located in Lumbini Cultural Municipality, Ward No. 11, Mahadewa, Rupandehi. We are committed to fostering the growth of Information Technology and education in Nepal.

Our center serves as a hub for skill development, where technology meets education, and innovation shapes the future of our community. We provide comprehensive services that cater to the needs of individuals in the digital age.

With experienced trainers, modern facilities, and affordable fees, we ensure that quality education is accessible to everyone in our community.

🎯 Our Mission

To empower individuals with digital skills, language proficiency, and professional IT services for a smarter future.

👁️ Our Vision

To become the leading provider of IT education in Nepal, creating a digitally empowered community.
